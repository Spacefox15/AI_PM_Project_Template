BEFORE implementing any technical choice.

1. Ask: What decision are you making?

3. Ask: Why did you choose this option?
4. Ask: What tradeoffs are you accepting?
5. Auto-assign the next DEC-### number.
6. Append the formatted entry to docs/decisions.md.
7. Confirm the entry was saved.
